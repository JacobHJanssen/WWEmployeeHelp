Pre-Shift Setup
  • Clock In
  • Check if cart is going out (only if course is busy; check Mid Shift if not going out)
  • Pull bar cart out
  • Load up beverages, liquors, mixers, shots, ice, scoop, snacks, cups, lids, straws, portion cups, trash bags, toilet paper
  • Refill specialty shots & lemonade if needed
  • Grab iPad with cover and log in to ForeUp App (ensure standard Wi-Fi is disconnected)
  • CRITICAL: Connect credit reader to iPad before heading out!
  • Obtain money bag from person with safe key & count to verify exactly $100
Course Etiquette & Sales
  • Go out and sell beverages & snacks
  • DRIVE SLOW: Watch your speed and bumps while operating on the course
  • DO NOT STOP in mid-fairway or sell when golfers are on green. Catch them on tee boxes!
  • Empty course garbage cans as needed
  • Restock portable toilets as needed
  • Watch for outside alcohol & underage drinkers (do not hesitate to card)
  • Monitor for unpaid golfers joining members and keep overall pace moving cleanly
Return & Post-Shift Support
  • Restock bar cart when done (shots, snacks, ice, drinks)
  • CLEAN CART: wipe down, empty trash, completely wash out the trash container, clean front cubbies
  • Park bar cart in shed and bring keys inside
  • Countdown money bag and input cash sale totals into main register
  • Put cash tips into bucket; split credit card tips among shift team
  • Drop cash bag with baseline $100 back into safe
  • Close ForeUp drawer on iPad, sign out, uncase it, and plug both units in to charge
  • Assist with cooking, serving inside, cleaning tables, throwing out trash, and putting fleet carts away
Opening Routines
  • Clock In, disarm alarm system & turn on facility lights
  • Bathrooms: unblock doors, turn on lights, ensure clean and stocked
  • Power on bar light signs (green extension cord under front window & e-tabs switch)
  • Unlock office, unlock safe, extract till & gaming trays, and count them
  • Fill out gaming sheet and place money tray securely into locked drawer
  • Log in to ForeUp, open register drawer, and embed money tray
  • Unlock back exit, walk-in cooler, and dry storage areas
  • Fill sub-counter ice bin; layout bar mats and brew coffee
  • Vacuum floors, sills, gaming cup holders; put away clean dishes
  • Pull out fleet carts and catalogue cart numbers on sign-out sheets
  • BEFORE LIGHTING GRILL: Empty the grill grease tray
  • Turn on fryer and grill (verify if fryer oil requires changing first—once a week)
  • SUNDAY RESTRICTION: Absolutely no alcohol sales before 8:00 AM
Mid-Shift Upkeep
  • Monitor/clean liquor racks, counter spaces, and under soda canisters
  • Deep clean kitchen: degrease walls, wipe fridges, freezer, microwaves, pizza oven
  • Clean hot dog rollers while hot using lemon juice on a wet towel; wash catch tray
  • Break down all cardboard boxes flatly before dumping
  • Clean gaming machines using microfiber cloths and Pledge Multi-Surface Only
  • Wash windows, cycle venue trash bins, swap coffee pots, and wash downstairs space
  • Pull, clean, and spray down incoming golf carts; restock pencils and scorecards
Station Shutdowns
  • Fill ice bin to side tracks, creating an empty hollow center for overnight ice production
  • Shut off fryer, grill, hot dog unit, pizza oven, ventilation hoods, and line fans
  • Clean grill: apply oil, scrub down with grill brick, wipe clean with towels and damp rag
  • Sanitize blender and clean slushie machine (knobs off into warm water cup, wash/dry trays and replace immediately)
  • Wash all mats, back dishes, prep tables, and hang cooler bags up to dry
Lockup & Facility Close
  • Secure all fleet carts inside storage barns; double check all rental keys are present!
  • Press "END CART RENTALS FOR THE DAY" on cart iPad and plug in to charge
  • Collapse, secure, and lock down large black outdoor patio umbrella
  • Clean, stock, and disinfect bathrooms; prop open doors and cut lights off
  • Check and empty all venue trash cans
  • Check recycling bins and take bags to the trailer behind the clubhouse
  • Check and take out small trash cans from the women's bathroom
  • Lock dry storage, walk-in cooler, lock back door, and secure with 2x4 block
  • Lock bonus room window barriers and secure with 2x4 block
  • Sweep and mop bar, lounge, and kitchen floor grids; unplug bar accent signs
Financial Closeout
  • Close gaming: audit tickets/cash, complete daily sheet balances, and initial
  • Confirm credit card tips are verified and fully uploaded into ForeUp sales
  • Count cash drawer down to baseline $300; submit final daily report, close all registers
  • Lock service window, kill laminator power, verify office safe is fully locked
  • Lock office entry using TOP DEADBOLT ONLY
  • Ensure ice cream freezer lids are tightly sealed
  • Arm building security alarm, kill remaining lights, lock entry door
Hot Food Prep Guide
  • Breakfast Sandwich
    Toast bun on grill. Cook Sausage, Egg patty and Bacon on Grill. Assemble and top with cheese on the bun.
  • Pizza Sticks
    Place in the deep fryer and cook until golden brown.
  • Taco in a Bag
    Use the large bags of Doritos (found on top of the beverage cooler), slice the bag open sideways. Microwave taco meat (found in fridge) for 1 minute, stir, and microwave again for 1 minute. Pour meat on top of chips, serve with lettuce and cheese (if available).
  • Breaded Chicken Sandwich
    Place chicken patty in the deep fryer, cook until the internal temperature is 185 degrees. Toast bun on griddle and assemble sandwich with cheese of choice.
  • Hot Dogs & Brats
    Located in the freezer. Place on hot dog roller on LOW setting. If no hot dogs/brats on roller, microwave for 1 minute and then cook on grill top for 1-2 minutes until ready. Toast hot dog buns on grill top.
  • Pizza
    Spray a baking pan with oil. Place pizza in pan and back at 350F, rotating every 5 minutes for roughly 10-15 minutes until pizza is cooked. Slice in half and serve as slices.
  • Chicken Strips
    Located in the freezer. Place in the deep fryer. Use the yellow meat thermometer to check each strip. Internal temperature must be at least 180 degrees!
  • Corn Dog
    Located in the freezer. Do NOT immediately place it in the deep fryer. Microwave TWO at a time at 30 second intervals until hot (usually 1.5 minutes). THEN place in the deep fryer for 2 minutes.
  • Hamburger / Cheeseburger
    Patties located in the freezer. Place patties on grill top. Season with burger magic seasoning. Cook for about 2-3 minutes per side on grill top and top with choice of cheese. Toast bun on grill and assemble. If topped with bacon, place bacon on griddle and cook for 2-3 minutes each side.
  • Poutine
    Drop french fries and HALF ORDER of cheese curds in the deep fryer. While frying, microwave brown gravy located in the fridge at 20 second intervals until hot. Assemble fries and cheese curds together and top with gravy.
  • Brisket Grilled Cheese
    Brisket located in the fridge. Take 1 piece of brisket (depending on the size) from the bag and place on the grill top. After that it is heated all the way through, placed to the side. Take two slices of bread from the fridge, butter and place down on the grill top. Add one slice of american cheese down on the bread and then the brisket on top, add some bbq sauce and add one more slice of american cheese on top.

ForeUp Navigation Steps

  1. Navigate to the Reports tab and select New Reporting.
  2. Scroll down and select the report titled Tee Times Sold.
  3. Select Example out of the choices under Tee Times Sold.
  4. Change the targeted date framework filter from Today to Today to refresh the active records.

Connecting Credit Reader to iPad

  1. Power on the credit reader device by hitting the GREEN button.
  2. Open iPad settings and make sure:
    • Wifi: ON (but NOT connected to any network)
    • Bluetooth: OFF
    • Personal Hotspot: ON!
  3. Type 0001 on the card reader pad to unlock the ADMIN MENU.
  4. Press option 2 to open WIFI PARAMETERS.
  5. Press option 3 to enter MY NETWORKS, then use the F3 key to scroll to DELETE ALL.
  6. Confirm deletion by hitting the GREEN button. Then hit the YELLOW button once to back out to Wifi Parameters.
  7. Press option 2 to Scan Networks. Select the corresponding iPad name (e.g., Bar Cart 1, Bar Cart 2, or League).
  8. Enter the password found directly in your iPad's Personal Hotspot setup page.
  9. Press the GREEN button to acknowledge the wifi link, wait for the green wifi status bars to show up on the device screen corner.
  10. Press the YELLOW and POUND (#) buttons simultaneously to reboot the terminal. Once dead, tap GREEN to power back on.
  11. Connection is successful when it boots up displaying "ForeUp Systems". Open ForeUp on the iPad and run a test cart transaction to confirm.
